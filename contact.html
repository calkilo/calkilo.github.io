Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI calorie calculation?

Our AI achieves 99.2% accuracy in calorie calculation by analyzing food photos using advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms trained on millions of food images.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and follow strict privacy protocols. Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, Calkilo works offline for basic features. However, AI-powered calorie calculation requires an internet connection for real-time processing.

Can I export my nutrition data?

Absolutely! You can export your nutrition data in various formats including CSV, PDF, and JSON through the app's settings menu.

What devices are supported?

Calkilo is available for iOS 12+ and Android 8+ devices. We support both phones and tablets with optimized interfaces for each device type.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account anytime through the app settings or by contacting our support team. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
