Getting Started

Calkilo is available for free download on both iOS and Android devices:

  • iOS: Visit the App Store and search for "Calkilo" or click the download button on our website
  • Android: Visit Google Play Store and search for "Calkilo" or use the download link on our website

Once downloaded, simply open the app and follow the setup wizard to create your account and start tracking your nutrition.

While you can use some basic features without an account, creating a free account allows you to:

  • Save your nutrition history and progress
  • Sync data across multiple devices
  • Set personalized health goals
  • Access advanced AI features
  • Export your data

Account creation is quick and only requires your email address and a password.

For the most accurate results, follow these tips when photographing your food:

  • Good lighting: Use natural light or bright, even lighting
  • Clear view: Make sure all food items are visible and not covered
  • Close-up: Fill the frame with your food, but keep some background visible for scale
  • Multiple angles: Take photos from different angles if the food has multiple layers
  • Clean background: Use a simple, contrasting background when possible
  • Fresh food: Photograph food when it's fresh and properly presented

Accuracy & AI Technology

Calkilo's AI achieves 99.2% accuracy in calorie calculation through:

  • Advanced Computer Vision: State-of-the-art image recognition technology
  • Machine Learning: Trained on millions of food images and nutritional data
  • Continuous Learning: AI improves with each photo analyzed
  • Portion Estimation: Sophisticated algorithms to estimate serving sizes
  • Food Recognition: Identifies thousands of foods and ingredients
  • Quality Control: Multiple validation layers ensure accuracy

While highly accurate, results are estimates and may vary based on food preparation methods and portion sizes.

Calkilo can recognize a wide variety of foods including:

  • Fresh Foods: Fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, dairy products
  • Prepared Meals: Salads, sandwiches, pasta, rice dishes, soups
  • Packaged Foods: Snacks, cereals, beverages, frozen foods
  • International Cuisine: Asian, Mediterranean, Latin American, and other global dishes
  • Complex Dishes: Multi-ingredient meals and recipes
  • Beverages: Coffee, tea, smoothies, alcoholic drinks

Our AI database contains over 100,000 food items and continues to grow with user contributions.

Calkilo uses advanced computer vision to estimate portion sizes through:

  • Reference Objects: Uses common items (plates, utensils, hands) for scale
  • 3D Modeling: Creates 3D models of food to estimate volume
  • Machine Learning: Learns from user corrections to improve estimates
  • Manual Adjustment: Users can adjust portion sizes after AI analysis
  • Standard Portions: Provides common serving size options

For best results, include reference objects in your photos and verify portion estimates.

Technical Support

Calkilo supports a wide range of devices and operating systems:

  • iOS: iPhone and iPad running iOS 12.0 or later
  • Android: Phones and tablets running Android 8.0 (API level 26) or later
  • Camera Requirements: Rear camera with autofocus for best photo quality
  • Storage: At least 100MB free space for app and data
  • Internet: Wi-Fi or cellular data for AI processing and sync
  • Offline Mode: Basic features work without internet connection

We regularly update the app to support the latest devices and operating systems.

If Calkilo is running slowly or crashing, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Restart the App: Close and reopen the app completely
  • Check Internet: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Update the App: Download the latest version from the app store
  • Clear Cache: Clear app cache in device settings
  • Free Storage: Ensure you have at least 500MB free storage
  • Restart Device: Restart your phone or tablet
  • Contact Support: If issues persist, contact our technical support team

Most performance issues are resolved with these simple steps.

Syncing your data across devices is automatic when you're signed in:

  • Account Required: Sign in with the same account on all devices
  • Automatic Sync: Data syncs automatically when connected to internet
  • Real-time Updates: Changes appear on all devices within minutes
  • Manual Sync: Pull down to refresh or go to Settings > Sync
  • Conflict Resolution: App handles conflicts by using the most recent data
  • Offline Changes: Changes sync when internet connection is restored

Ensure all devices are updated to the latest app version for best sync performance.
